Integrate Numici with Slack

Highlight, annotate and converse in context in web pages and PDF documents.

Numici offers Taskspace, essentially a workspace for the relevant documents for a specific task at hand, for example, writing a research report. As you read any document in Numici, you can highlight key information, take notes, and add tags for future discovery. You can Collaborate in Context - engage in conversations with your team around highlights and annotations.

With Slack integration turned on, the conversations and threads are transparently posted to the appropriate Slack channel, enabling an individual or a team to use standard Slack app to stay on top on conversations within Numici.
